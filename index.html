THE CASTELLAN TOWER PROLOGUE

TWO PATHS • ONE FATE

Ten years ago, Cipher and Vale were the city’s finest intelligence partners until a syndicate corruption scandal tore them apart. Vale chose the badge and the law; Cipher chose the shadows and vigilante justice.

Tonight, their paths collide at Castellan Tower. Cipher breached the penthouse for the Azure Diamond—the final evidence to expose the syndicate—and set a timed charge on Floor 1 to cover their escape. Vale decoded the alert and charged into the lobby, desperate to disarm the bomb and stop his former partner before either reaches the point of no return.

Cipher prepares to descend 40 floors. Vale rushes upward to disarm the device. Neither will yield—how this story ends depends on you.
